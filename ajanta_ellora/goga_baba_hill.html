Goga Baba Hills

Info

Goga Baba Hill is a tranquil location.This site is favourite among trekkers.After trekking half an hour one can easily reach at the top of the hill.The panoramic view from the top of the hill spellbound the tourists.One can even see the gorgeous view of the sunset. At the top of hill there is a small white coloured temple.


Reachability

Situated almost 17 km from Aurangabad city, goga baba hill can be reached easily via buses and taxis. The drive will take you around an 20 min to reach the hill.
