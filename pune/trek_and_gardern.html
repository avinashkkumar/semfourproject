Sinhagad Fort

Info

Sinhagad is a hill fortress located at around 35 km southwest of the city of Pune, India. Some of the information available at this fort suggests that the fort could have been built 2000 years ago. The caves and the carvings in the Kaundinyeshwar temple stand as proofs for the same.

Reachability

Sinhagad fort :The nearest railway station Pune Railway Station, located about 40km from the Fort. Travellers can take cabs to reach the fort, or even opt for government operated buses from Pune station/airport. Your journey from Pune station to Sinhagad will be for an hour and 15 minutes.

Average visiting time 3 Hours
Visiting Hours 9am–5:30pm
Ticket ₹ 50

Pune-Okayama Friendship Garden

Info

Pune-Okayama Friendship Garden or Pu La Deshpande Udyan is one of the largest gardens in Pune located on Sinhagad Road in Pune, Maharashtra, India.

Reachability

Pune-Okayama Friendship Garden :From Pune Railway Station, located about 9km from the Garden. Travellers can take cabs to reach the Garden, or even opt for government operated buses from Pune station/airport. Your journey from Pune station to Garden will be for an 28 minutes.

Average visiting time 3 Hours
Visiting Hours 6:30–10:30am, 4–8pm
Ticket ₹ 50
