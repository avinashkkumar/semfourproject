Khadakwasla

Khadakwasla

Info

Khadakwasla Dam is a dam on the Mutha River 21 km (13 mi) from the centre of the city of Pune in Maharashtra, India. The dam created a reservoir known as Khadakwasla Lake which is the main source of water for Pune and its suburbs.

Reachability

Khadakwasla :- Pune Railway Station, located about 17km from the dam. Travellers can take cabs to reach the fort, or even opt for buses from Pune station/airport. Your journey from Pune station to Khadakwasla will be for an hour.

Average visiting time 3 Hours
Ticket ₹ 50

Pashan Lake

Info

Pashan Lake has gained a lot of popularity as a major tourist spot in Pune. The beauty and serenity offered by this lake captivate and lure a host of photo fanatics and bird lovers to visit the place frequently. In addition to tourists, the pleasant atmosphere and green surroundings also propel many migratory birds to the lake each year.

Reachability

Pashan lake :- Pune Railway Station, located about 13-15km from the Lake. Travellers can take cabs to reach the lake, or even opt for government operated buses from Pune station/airport. Your journey from Pune station to Lake will be for an 40 minutes.

Average visiting time 3 Hours
Ticket ₹ 50
